--- title: Start and End ---

The circumflex has a second meaning when it is used outside of the brackets. When use the circumflex ^ outside of brackets, it matches the beginning of the line.

The example with the numbers 20-29 should be solvable much shorter, now. Place the circumflex at the beginning of the regular expression to find all numbers starting with 2.
