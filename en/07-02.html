--- title: Start and End ---

Circumflexes can be combined with sets. Place the circumflex to find all fruits that to not start with letters from C to G.

  • Apple
  • Banana
  • Cherry
  • Date
  • Elderberry
  • Fig
  • Grapefruit
  • Ginger
  • Hops
  • Jasmin
  • Lemon

With ^[^C-G], we look for all lines, which do not start with C, D, E, F or G.
