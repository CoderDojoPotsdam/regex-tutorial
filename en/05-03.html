--- title: Sets with Ranges ---

You can write ranges of letters with - in a short way. You can find all capital letters from A to Z using [A-Z]. Mark all meals with a white background. You only need five characters.

  • Apple
  • Banana
  • Cherry
  • Date
  • Elderberry
  • Fig
  • Grapefruit
  • Ginger
  • Hops
  • Jasmin
  • Lemon
